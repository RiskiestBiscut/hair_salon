Meet our Team

Sophia Rivera

Master Stylist & Creative Visionary

With over a decade of experience, Sophia Rivera is a seasoned stylist known for her creative flair and precision. Trained in the latest trends and techniques, Sophia specializes in crafting personalized looks that showcase her clients' individuality. Her passion for the artistry of hair is evident in every cut, color, and style she creates, making her a sought-after expert in the world of beauty.

Elijah Hayes

Color Specialist & Trendsetter

Elijah Hayes is not just a hairstylist; he's a trendsetter in the world of hair color. With an eye for detail and a keen sense of color theory, Elijah transforms ordinary hair into vibrant works of art. His expertise ranges from classic highlights to avant-garde color techniques, and he takes pride in staying ahead of the curve in the ever-evolving world of hair fashion.

Ava Mitchell

Texture Maven & Wellness Advocate

Ava Mitchell is your go-to stylist for all things texture and wellness. Specializing in smoothing treatments, perms, and rejuvenating hair and scalp therapies, Ava is dedicated to enhancing both the beauty and health of her clients' hair. With a focus on natural beauty and holistic care, Ava brings a refreshing approach to the Shear Elegance experience.

Miles Rodriguez

Men's Grooming Expert & Barber Extraordinaire

Miles Rodriguez is a master of men's grooming, seamlessly blending classic techniques with modern styles. With a background in traditional barbering, Miles delivers precision haircuts and tailored grooming services, creating sharp, confident looks for every client. His attention to detail and commitment to providing a personalized experience make him a go-to stylist for discerning gentlemen.
